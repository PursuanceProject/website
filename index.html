Pursuance

Impactful, agile, secure civic collaboration

Pursuance enables many individuals to organize in the pursuit of one defined goal.


Individuals and/or existing organizations form or join a “pursuance,” a process-oriented group structured by a highly customizable framework for collaboration, to work toward a common goal.

Pursuances combine both task management and discussion functionality, allowing new members to quickly get up to speed and enabling productive collaboration throughout a team.

Pursuances can interact with each other, cooperating on shared goals or assigning tasks to one another.

Pursuance makes the collaborative efforts of all kinds of decentralized groups more effective.



Independent activists can organize quickly and effectively.


Nonprofits and charities can engage more meaningfully with volunteers.


Journalists and researchers can collaborate on major stories and projects.

Pursuance is currently being developed!
